CPR Knowledge Test

Whether or not you would want CPR (cardiopulmonary resuscitation) attempted on you is one of the most important medical decisions you’ll face. Take this “test” to learn some facts about CPR that might surprise you!

It doesn’t matter if you get the answers to this test right or wrong—the intention is to think about CPR in a way that might be new for you.

Question 1:

Most people who receive CPR can return to their daily activities within a day or two.

False: in reality, less than 10% of adults who survive CPR will return to their previous quality of life. Most people who receive CPR will have to be on a ventilator, or breathing machine.
